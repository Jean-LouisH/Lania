Initial configuration

* Is in the form of "Init.cfg" for project directories. 
* Defines data to initiate before the application loads, such as window configuration.

keywords;

For application window.

title 		-  to appear on the window.
resolution 		- in the format of "WidthxHeight" 
rendering_api 	- from either "OpenGL" or "Vulkan"
fullscreen 		- "false", "true"
resizable		- "false", "true"
maximized		- "false", "true"
main_scene		- The first scene to load. Referenced as "x.lscn".
